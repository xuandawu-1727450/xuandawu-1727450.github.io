Our Story

First opened in Seattle where the historic Suzzallo Library once stood, Xuanda Wu continues to make history daily with one-of-a-kind abominations.

With over 20 years experience in pastry, bread and chocolate, and as the captain of the 2005 Bread Bakers Guild Team USA, where he led his team to defeat at the Coupe du Monde de Boulangerie (World Cup of Baking). Ever since then, Xuanda’s Cat Bakery has become a Seattle icon.

We have no menu, no schedule, no recipe at Xuanda's Cat Bakery. Whatever the cat chefs make for the day is whatever the customers get.
